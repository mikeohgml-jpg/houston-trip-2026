🛂 Visa & US Entry
✅
Great news! Singapore is on the US Visa Waiver Program (VWP). You do not need a US visa. You need to apply for ESTA instead — it's quick and cheap.

📋 ESTA – What You Need to Know

Official Siteesta.cbp.dhs.gov
CostUSD $21 per person
ProcessingUsually instant (allow up to 72 hrs)
Validity2 years or until passport expiry
Max stayUp to 90 days per trip
How earlyApply at least 2–3 weeks before travel
Apply ESTA Now →

⚠️ Critical Points

  • Apply separately for both of you — Arielle needs her own ESTA as a minor
  • Use ONLY the official esta.cbp.dhs.gov site — many scam sites charge more
  • Print your ESTA approval confirmation and bring it to check-in
  • ESTA does NOT guarantee entry — final decision is by CBP officer at the airport
  • If either ESTA is denied, you'd need to apply for a B-2 visa (allow 4–6 weeks)

📱 Recommended App: Mobile Passport

Download the Mobile Passport Control app (free, official US government app) before departing Singapore. This allows faster processing at US Customs by submitting your passport and declaration digitally before you land. Works for foreign nationals with ESTA.
